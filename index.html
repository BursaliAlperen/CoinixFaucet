Frequently Asked Questions
How do I earn CNX?
Claim the faucet every 3 minutes for free CNX, complete offerwall tasks, or invite friends to earn referral bonuses.
How do I withdraw?
Swap your CNX to DOGE using the live market rate, then withdraw to your FaucetPay email. Minimum withdrawal is 0.10 DOGE.
What is CNX worth?
1 CNX is pegged at $0.01 USD. You can swap CNX to DOGE at any time using the live DOGE market price.
What is the referral bonus?
You earn 1 CNX for every friend who joins using your link, plus a 20% lifetime commission on all their faucet and offerwall earnings.
Is there a return bonus?
Yes! If you are inactive for 3 or more days, you will receive a welcome back bonus of 1 CNX when you return.
