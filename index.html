❓Frequently Asked Questions
How do I earn coins? ▼

Claim the faucet every 10 seconds for free DOGE, PEPE, POL, FEYORRA and DGB. Each coin has its own 100 daily claim limit. You also earn CNX at every 25, 250, 2500... claim milestone.

How do I withdraw? ▼

Go to Withdraw page, choose a coin, enter your FaucetPay email and amount. Minimum is $0.03 USD worth of any coin. Withdrawals are processed manually by admin.

What is the milestone bonus? ▼

Every 25 claims you get 0.10 CNX. At 250 claims you get 1 CNX. At 2500 claims you get 10 CNX, and so on forever. There is no limit!

What is CNX worth? ▼

1 CNX is pegged at $0.01 USD. You can swap CNX to DOGE or convert other coins to CNX at any time.

Is there a captcha? ▼

Yes, a simple math captcha protects every coin claim. No annoying image puzzles or ReCaptcha required.
