You can claim free cryptocurrency every 60 seconds, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. There is no daily limit.

FaucetPay is a micro-wallet service that enables instant cryptocurrency withdrawals. All CoinixFaucet payouts are sent instantly to your FaucetPay email.

Absolutely. CoinixFaucet is protected by Cloudflare, uses JWT authentication, encrypted passwords, XSS protection, rate limiting and Firestore security rules.
