FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I earn CNX?
  • Claiming the faucet every 3 minutes (1 CNX)
  • Completing Offerwall tasks - PTC ads, games, website visits, shortlinks (+20% bonus)
  • Referral program - invite friends and earn +50 CNX per signup
How do I withdraw?
  1. Earn CNX from faucet and offers.
  2. Go to Swap and convert CNX to DOGE (1:1, no fees).
  3. Go to Withdraw page.
  4. Enter your FaucetPay email and the DOGE amount.
  5. Minimum withdrawal is 0.10 DOGE.
  6. The admin processes your payout and DOGE arrives on your FaucetPay account.
What is CNX worth in USD?
100 CNX = 1 USD. So 1 CNX = $0.01. The USD value updates in real-time based on the current DOGE price. You can see the current USD value and 24h price change percentage next to your balance in the header.
What tasks are available on Offerwall?
Offerwall.me provides 4 types of tasks:
  • PTC (Paid-to-Click) - Watch ads and click links
  • Website Visits - Visit websites for a specified time
  • Games - Install and play games to reach certain levels
  • Shortlinks - Visit shortened URLs
All rewards are credited automatically via secure server-to-server postback. PTC, Shortlinks and Games earn an extra +20% bonus on top of the base reward!
How does referral bonus work?
When someone signs up with your referral link, you earn 50 CNX instantly. Additionally, you earn 20% commission on all their faucet claims and offerwall earnings. Referral earnings are stored separately in your Referral Earnings balance. You can collect them anytime by clicking the Collect Referral Bonus button on the Withdraw page.
How long does withdrawal take?
Most withdrawals are processed within 24 hours.
