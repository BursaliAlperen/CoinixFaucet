❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I earn CNX? ▼
  • Claiming the faucet every 3 minutes (0.20 CNX)
  • Completing Offerwall tasks - PTC ads, games, website visits, shortlinks
  • Referral program — invite friends and earn +50 CNX per signup
How do I withdraw? ▼
  1. Earn CNX from faucet and offers.
  2. Go to Swap and convert CNX → DOGE (1:1, no fees).
  3. Go to Withdraw page.
  4. Enter your FaucetPay email and the DOGE amount.
  5. Minimum withdrawal is 0.10 DOGE.
  6. The admin processes your payout and DOGE arrives on your FaucetPay account.
What is CNX worth in USD? ▼
CNX is pegged 1:1 to DOGE. The USD value updates in real-time based on the current DOGE price. You can see the current USD value and 24h price change percentage next to your balance in the header.
What tasks are available on Offerwall? ▼
Offerwall.me provides 4 types of tasks:
  • PTC (Paid-to-Click) - Watch ads and click links
  • Website Visits - Visit websites for a specified time
  • Games - Install and play games to reach certain levels
  • Shortlinks - Visit shortened URLs
All rewards are credited automatically via secure server-to-server postback.
How long does withdrawal take? ▼
Most withdrawals are processed within 24 hours. You'll receive your DOGE on your FaucetPay account.
