FAQ · Frequently Asked
How do I earn CNX?
You can earn CNX by:
• Claiming the faucet every 10 seconds
• Watching PTC ads and completing offers on the PTC / Tasks page
• Referral program — invite friends and earn +50 CNX per signup
How do I withdraw?
1. Earn CNX from the faucet and ads.
2. Go to Swap and convert CNX → DOGE (1:1, no fees).
3. Go to Withdraw page.
4. Enter your FaucetPay email and the DOGE amount.
5. Minimum withdrawal is 0.10 DOGE.
6. The admin processes your payout and DOGE arrives on your FaucetPay account.
Do you only accept FaucetPay email for withdrawals?
Yes. To keep things simple, withdrawals are sent via FaucetPay only. Please enter the email address you registered with on FaucetPay. DOGE wallet addresses and usernames are not supported.

Don't have a FaucetPay account? Create one here — it's free.
How does the referral program work?
Share your unique referral link with friends. When they sign up via your link, you receive +50 CNX automatically. There's no limit on referrals — invite as many friends as you like.
How long does withdrawal take?
Most withdrawals are processed within 24 hours. You can check the status of every withdrawal under the History tab on the Withdraw page.
What is the exchange rate CNX → DOGE?
The swap rate is 1 CNX = 1 DOGE with no fees. You can swap back and forth anytime on the Swap page.
